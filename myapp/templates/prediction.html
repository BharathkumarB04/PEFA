📌 How Does This Work?

Our Expense Predictor uses Machine Learning (ML) to analyze your past spending and predict next month’s total expense.


🔍 Technology Behind the Prediction

  • ✅ Data Aggregation: We collect and analyze past expenses month-wise.
  • ✅ Linear Regression Model: A simple ML model that predicts future expenses based on past spending trends.
  • ✅ Self-Learning: As you enter more expenses, the model improves over time.


🚀 Benefits of Expense Prediction

  • ✔ Budget Planning: Helps you plan your finances in advance.
  • ✔ Spending Insights: Understand if your expenses are increasing or decreasing.
  • ✔ Smart Savings: Adjust your budget based on expected future expenses.


🛠️ How is This Implemented in PeFA?

We use past 3+ months of expenses to train a Machine Learning model. The model predicts next month’s expense based on spending patterns. This prediction is updated automatically every month.


⚠️ Notes & Considerations

  • 🔹 Accuracy depends on data: More past data = better predictions.
  • 🔹 Does not factor external events: Sudden financial changes (e.g., unexpected purchases) are not considered.
  • 🔹 A simple model: We use a lightweight approach to ensure fast performance.
